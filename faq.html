What are these acronyms your using? Like CSU, UC,...
  • UC: University of California. This is a public school system in California with 9 campuses, one of which is exclusively a health science campus (UC San Francisco)
  • CSU: California State University. This is similar to other state schools. Another public school system with 23 campuses.
  • IGETC: Intersegmental General Education Transfer Curriculum. A list of classes that are needed to transfer. It's basically a checklist of classes that ensures you've completed a certain number of units in certain subject areas. The IGETC covers both UC's and CSU's.
